# 6. PLATFORM DISCLAIMER RULE

My Gujarat Property should clearly state:

```txt id="4vlkcb"
My Gujarat Property is a property discovery, listing, advertising and lead-generation platform. Users must independently verify property documents, ownership, pricing, RERA details and legal status before making any payment or decision.
```

Also state:

```txt id="s6f8re"
Platform payments are for listings, subscriptions, advertisements, boosts and platform services only. The platform does not collect property purchase token, booking amount or sale consideration unless a future legally approved transaction system is implemented.
```

Do not imply the platform guarantees property transaction safety.
